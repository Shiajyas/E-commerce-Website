CCTV KNOWLEDGE BASE
========================================

========================
GENERAL
========================

What is CCTV?

CCTV stands for Closed-Circuit Television.
It is a surveillance system used to monitor homes, offices, shops, warehouses, schools, hospitals, banks, and public places.

========================
CAMERAS
========================

What is a Bullet Camera?

A Bullet Camera is a long cylindrical camera mainly used for outdoor surveillance.
It usually provides longer viewing distance and weather protection.

What is a Dome Camera?

A Dome Camera has a dome-shaped housing.
It is commonly used indoors because it is compact and difficult to identify its viewing direction.

What is an IP Camera?

An IP Camera captures video digitally and transmits it through a computer network using an IP address.

What is an Analog Camera?

An Analog Camera sends video through coaxial cable to a DVR.

========================
NVR
========================

What is NVR?

NVR stands for Network Video Recorder.

An NVR records video from IP cameras over a network.

NVR does not require direct video cable connections like DVR.

Advantages of NVR

- Better image quality
- Easy remote access
- Supports PoE
- Easy camera expansion

========================
DVR
========================

What is DVR?

DVR stands for Digital Video Recorder.

A DVR records video from Analog Cameras.

Video signals are transmitted through coaxial cables.

Advantages

- Lower installation cost
- Suitable for existing analog systems

========================
NVR VS DVR
========================

Difference between DVR and NVR

DVR works with Analog Cameras.

NVR works with IP Cameras.

DVR uses coaxial cables.

NVR uses Ethernet network cables.

NVR usually supports higher resolution cameras.

========================
POE
========================

What is PoE?

PoE stands for Power over Ethernet.

PoE allows both power and data to travel through one Ethernet cable.

Advantages

- Fewer cables
- Easy installation
- Lower installation cost
- Cleaner wiring

========================
RESOLUTION
========================

What is 2MP Camera?

A 2MP camera captures approximately 1920 × 1080 resolution.

It is commonly known as Full HD.

What is 4MP Camera?

A 4MP camera provides better image quality than 2MP.

What is 8MP Camera?

An 8MP camera is also called 4K CCTV Camera.

========================
LENS
========================

What is Fixed Lens?

A Fixed Lens provides one viewing angle.

What is Varifocal Lens?

A Varifocal Lens allows manual zoom adjustment.

========================
NIGHT VISION
========================

What is Infrared?

Infrared LEDs allow cameras to capture images in darkness.

What is Color Night Vision?

Color Night Vision allows full-color recording even in low light.

========================
AI FEATURES
========================

What is Motion Detection?

Motion Detection starts recording whenever movement is detected.

What is Human Detection?

Human Detection identifies people while reducing false alarms.

What is Vehicle Detection?

Vehicle Detection recognizes moving vehicles.

What is Face Detection?

Face Detection automatically detects human faces.

========================
VIDEO
========================

What is H.264?

H.264 is a video compression standard.

What is H.265?

H.265 provides better compression than H.264 while maintaining video quality.

========================

192.168.1.64

or

192.168.1.108

========================
STORAGE
========================

How long can CCTV footage be stored?

Storage depends on

- Hard Disk Size
- Camera Resolution
- Frame Rate
- Number of Cameras
- Recording Mode

A 1TB hard disk generally stores 10 to 20 days of footage for four Full HD cameras.

INSTALLATION
========================

How to install CCTV Camera?

Step 1

Choose the installation location.

Step 2

Mount the camera.

Step 3

Connect power.

Step 4

Connect Ethernet cable or coaxial cable.

Step 5

Connect DVR or NVR.

Step 6

Configure network settings.

Step 7

Add camera to DVR or NVR.

Step 8

Test live video.

========================
RESET
========================

How to reset a CCTV Camera?

Press and hold the reset button for about 10 to 15 seconds.

Wait for the device to reboot.

========================
TROUBLESHOOTING
========================

Camera not showing video

Possible reasons

- No power
- Loose cable
- Wrong IP address
- Camera disabled
- Network issue

No Night Vision

Possible reasons

- Infrared disabled
- Dirty lens
- Hardware failure

Cannot access camera remotely

Check

- Internet connection
- Port forwarding
- DDNS
- Hik-Connect
- Correct login credentials

========================
MAINTENANCE
========================

How often should CCTV cameras be cleaned?

Clean the camera lens every three months.

Inspect cables every six months.

Check hard disk health once a year.

========================
FAQ
========================

Can CCTV work without Internet?

Yes.

Internet is only required for remote viewing.

Can CCTV work during power failure?

Only if connected to UPS or backup power.

Can I view CCTV on my mobile?

Yes.

Using applications like Hik-Connect or CP Plus mobile app.

Can one NVR support multiple cameras?

Yes.

Depending on model, an NVR may support 4, 8, 16, 32 or more cameras.

How many cameras can a DVR support?

Common DVR models support

4

8

16

32

or 64 cameras.
